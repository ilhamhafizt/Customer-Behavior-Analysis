Workflow
Project Workflow
1
Dataset Generation & Data Understanding
A synthetic dataset of 5,000 transactions from 1,000 customers was created with a realistic purchase distribution (20% heavy buyers). Data intentionally includes dirty records: null dates, negative quantities, corrupt product names, and duplicates.
🐍 Python · NumPy · random
2
Data Cleaning dengan Python & Pandas
Script removes duplicates, rows with null dates, negative quantities, and invalid product names. The revenue column is calculated after cleaning. Output: 4,312 clean transactions.
🧹 Pandas · NumPy · Regex
3
RFM Segmentation with Pandas
Each customer is scored on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary using quintile scoring (1–5). Score combinations produce 7 strategic segments: Champions, Loyal Customers, Potential Loyalists, Recent Customers, At Risk, Can't Lose Them, and Lost Customers.
🗄️ RFM Model · pd.qcut · groupby
4
Monthly Cohort Retention Analysis
Customers are grouped by their first transaction month (cohort). Retention rates are calculated relative to cohort size at M+0. A Matplotlib/Seaborn heatmap visualizes churn patterns over time.
🔁 Cohort · pivot_table · Seaborn Heatmap
Insight
Key Findings
💎
Champions are 18.7% but drive 41% of revenue
The Champions segment (187 customers) averages Rp 18.4M per customer — 8.7x higher than Lost Customers. An exclusive loyalty program for this segment could significantly boost revenue.
📉
Retention drops 60%+ in the first month (M+1)
On average, only 38% of customers return in the second month, indicating weak onboarding. Re-engagement strategies like personalized emails or M+1 discounts could dramatically improve retention.
🔄
150 'Lost' customers can still be reactivated
The Lost Customers segment (15%) has a historically decent monetary value. A win-back campaign with exclusive offers could recover roughly 20–30% of this segment.
📈
Revenue grew 11.4% YoY from 2023 to 2024
Despite seasonal dips (Feb–Mar), the overall trend is positive. Revenue peaks in December — a strong indicator of seasonal buying behavior that can be leveraged with year-end campaigns.
